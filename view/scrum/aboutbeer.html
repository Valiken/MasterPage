About Page

Who we aim to serve:

We aim to serve men and women over the age of 21 who love beer. Whether its a golden amber ale or a dark rich stout. This site brings them all together to talk about their favorite activities and their favorite beer!

What is this site about?

This site is dedicated to the love, fascination, and pursuit of the perfect beer.

Why we are doing this:

Why are we doing this? Simple. Because everyone loves beer. Whether you drink once a year, a month, or once a day this site is for you. So that you can find the best beer in town and to find the next up and coming brew or activity.
