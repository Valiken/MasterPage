Hobbies & Interests

My hobbies and interests are rather varied. I love to cook different types of food from different cultures, including, but not limited to; Mexican, Chinese, German, and American. Honestly I love to eat almost as much as I love to cook. One of styles of food that I make the most is traditional American style bbq, not to be confused with grilling a steak. This bbq is the type that takes 12+ hours to cook in a smoker over low heat. The smoker we use is a Weber Smokey Mountain. Mainly I make pulled pork and beef brisket. I’ve almost perfected my pulled pork recipe; however, the brisket has a long way to go until I am happy with it.

Another one of my hobbies is gardening, since I live with my parents I have a large yard to work with. Currently the plots of land that we have set aside for farming are currently in a fallow period, where nothing is growing. I also have a Japanese style garden that is a work in progress in the back of our property. We have many trees such as Lemon, Lime, Tangerine, and an Apple tree. The reason I started to get into gardening is because I’ve found that you can usually grow something that tastes better at home than you can buy in a store.
