About Me

My name is Brian Berg and I have lived in California all of my life. Specifically I have lived in Burbank; however I was born in Glendale. I started my college education in 2007 at Glendale Community College where I took numerous science classes and finally decided that I wanted to go into a computer related field. I met some great teachers there who helped me along and shaped where I wanted to go when I transferred out to pursue a bachelor’s degree in Computer Information Systems. One of my friends, along with one of my teachers, suggested that I checked out Cal Poly Pomona as a possible school choice, along with CSU Northridge. What made me choose Cal Poly was the Learn by doing mentality that you really can’t find in a lot of other schools. Since then I have been at Cal Poly for almost two years now and I am planning on finishing up my classes and receiving my degree in fall of 2013. Hopefully after that I will be able to find a job that I really love and maybe that job will even be with your company!
